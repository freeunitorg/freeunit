# Straggler metaphor

"Straggler" — a slang term for developers who continue to use the old NGINX Unit, have not switched to the FreeUnit fork, and are unaware of freeunit.org's existence.

Why: the user coined this term to describe the target audience that needs to be brought into the FreeUnit community.

How to apply: use "straggler" as a label for content, discussions, or materials aimed at those who haven't yet switched to the fork.
